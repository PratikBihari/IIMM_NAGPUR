Chairman's Message

A warm welcome from our leadership

Dr. Y. Venkata Ramana

Chairman, IIMM Nagpur Branch

Excellence in materials management education is our commitment

Dear Members and Visitors,

It is my pleasure to welcome you to the Indian Institute of Materials Management (IIMM), Nagpur Branch website. For over 25 years, our branch has been at the forefront of materials management and supply chain education in the region.

In today's rapidly evolving business landscape, the role of materials and supply chain management has become increasingly critical for organizational success. At IIMM Nagpur, we are committed to developing professionals who can navigate these challenges with expertise and innovation.

Our programs are designed to provide both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, preparing our students for leadership roles in the industry. With a dedicated faculty of industry experts and a strong focus on real-world application, we ensure that our graduates are ready to make meaningful contributions to their organizations.

I invite you to explore our website, learn about our educational offerings, and join us in our mission to advance the field of materials management in India.

Dr. Y. Venkata Ramana

Chairman, IIMM Nagpur Branch
