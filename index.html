About Us

Learn more about IIMM Nagpur Branch

Our Vision

To be a premier institution for excellence in supply chain and materials management education, recognized globally for developing industry leaders.

Our Mission

To impart quality education and training in materials management, supply chain management, and related fields, ensuring our students become industry-ready professionals equipped with the knowledge and skills required in today's global business environment.

History

The Indian Institute of Materials Management (IIMM) Nagpur Branch has been serving the educational needs of aspiring supply chain professionals since its establishment. With a strong foundation in materials management principles and modern supply chain practices, we have continuously evolved our curriculum to meet industry demands.
