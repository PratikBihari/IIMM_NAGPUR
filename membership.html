Why Join IIMM

IIMM offers membership to professionals and businesses throughout India. Becoming a member demonstrates high standards and commitment to excellence in Supply Chain Management.

Professional Development

Carry out duties, develop knowledge, and stay current with trends

Networking

Connect with SCM practitioners, CPOs, and CEOs

Competitions

Participate in debates, quizzes, and professional contests

Individual Membership

  • Member directory & networking
  • Exclusive forums & lectures
  • Monthly newsletter & MMR magazine
  • Conference discounts (SCALE, NATCOM)
  • Fellow/Distinguish Member grades

Corporate Membership

  • Business directory promotion
  • Free job advertising
  • 2 free individual memberships
  • Discounted education & consulting
  • Industry & MSME events
