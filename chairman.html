Chairman's Message

A warm welcome from our leadership

Dr. Y. Venkata Ramana

Chairman, IIMM Nagpur Branch

Dear Members and Visitors,

It is my pleasure to welcome you to the Indian Institute of Materials Management (IIMM), Nagpur Branch website. For over 25 years, our branch has been at the forefront of materials management and supply chain education in the region.

In today's rapidly evolving business landscape, the role of materials and supply chain management has become increasingly critical for organizational success. At IIMM Nagpur, we are committed to developing professionals who can navigate these challenges with expertise and innovation.

Our programs are designed to provide both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, preparing our students for leadership roles in the industry. With a dedicated faculty of industry experts and a strong focus on real-world application, we ensure that our graduates are ready to make meaningful contributions to their organizations.

We take pride in our AICTE-approved Post Graduate Diploma programs in Materials Management (PGDMM) and Logistics & Supply Chain Management (PGDLSCM), which have consistently produced industry-ready professionals. Our curriculum is regularly updated to reflect the latest trends and technologies in supply chain management, including digitalization, sustainability, and global logistics.

The Nagpur Branch serves as a hub for professional development, offering networking opportunities, industry seminars, and continuous learning programs for both students and working professionals. Our strong industry connections ensure that our members stay connected with the latest developments and career opportunities in the field.

I invite you to explore our website, learn about our educational offerings, and join us in our mission to advance the field of materials management in India. Whether you are a student seeking quality education, a professional looking to enhance your skills, or an organization seeking partnership opportunities, IIMM Nagpur is here to support your journey.

Together, let us build a stronger supply chain community and contribute to India's economic growth.

Warm regards,
Dr. Y. Venkata Ramana
Chairman, IIMM Nagpur Branch

Chairman's Profile

Professional Background

Dr. Y. Venkata Ramana currently serves as General Manager (Materials Management) and Head of Department at Western Coalfields Limited (WCL) Material Management Department. He brings over three decades of experience in materials management, supply chain operations, and academic leadership. His extensive background spans both industry and academia, making him uniquely positioned to lead IIMM Nagpur Branch.

Key Achievements

  • Led the establishment of industry-academia partnerships with leading manufacturing and logistics companies
  • Instrumental in securing AICTE approval for PGDMM and PGDLSCM programs
  • Organized numerous national conferences and workshops on supply chain management
  • Mentored hundreds of professionals who now hold leadership positions in various industries
  • Published research papers on supply chain optimization and materials management

Vision for IIMM Nagpur

Under Dr. Ramana's leadership, IIMM Nagpur Branch aims to become the premier center for supply chain education in Central India, fostering innovation, research, and industry collaboration while maintaining the highest standards of academic excellence and professional development.
