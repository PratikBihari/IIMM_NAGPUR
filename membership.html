Membership

Join the IIMM community and advance your professional growth

Why Join IIMM

IIMM offers membership to professionals and businesses throughout India. Becoming a member demonstrates high standards and commitment to excellence in Supply Chain Management.

Professional Development

Carry out duties, develop knowledge, and stay current with trends

Networking

Connect with SCM practitioners, CPOs, and CEOs

Competitions

Participate in debates, quizzes, and professional contests

Individual Membership Types

Associate Member

Age: 18 years

Qualification: University degree/diploma in Engineering or Materials Management course

Experience: 1 year

Entrance Fees: ₹500/-

Annual Subscription: ₹500/-

Full Member

Age: 23 years

Qualification: University degree/diploma in Engineering or recognized Materials Management course

Experience: 2 years

Entrance Fees: ₹500/-

Annual Subscription: ₹1,000/-

Life Member

Age: 23 years

Qualification: University degree/diploma in Engineering or recognized Materials Management course

Experience: 2 years

Entrance Fees: ₹500/-

One-time Fees: ₹12,000/-

Fellow Member

Age: 40 years

Qualification: Senior status in Materials Management, University degree/diploma in Engineering

Experience: 15 years

Entrance Fees: ₹500/-

Annual Subscription: ₹3,000/-

Life Fellow Member

Eligibility: Life Member of IIMM

Entrance Fees: ₹500/-

Annual Subscription: ₹25,000/-

Institutional Membership Types

Large Scale

Eligibility: Organization with paid-up capital > ₹1 crore or annual turnover > ₹50 crores

Representatives: Two

Entrance Fees: ₹1,000/-

Annual Subscription: ₹6,000/-

Small Scale

Eligibility: Any firm, corporation, institute or enterprise with SSI registration

Representatives: One

Entrance Fees: ₹500/-

Annual Subscription: ₹2,500/-

Others

Eligibility: Any institute not falling in above categories

Representatives: One

Entrance Fees: ₹500/-

Annual Subscription: ₹1,500/-
